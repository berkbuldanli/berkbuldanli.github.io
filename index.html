About

Systems thinker, turned product manager.

I'm a product manager at Delivery Hero, working on marketplace platforms that operate in more than 20 countries across APAC, Europe, and MENA. My background is in industrial engineering, which is probably why I still think in systems and sequences before I think in features.

I started in product at Yemeksepeti — Türkiye's largest food-delivery marketplace — where I helped move two decades of company data and tooling onto Delivery Hero's global platform. Today I focus on the global ratings & reviews domain, shipping features that help millions of customers make better decisions across dozens of markets.

I care about discovery grounded in the real job a customer is trying to get done, decisions backed by data, and — above all — shipping things that actually make it to production.

Platform Migration

Migrating 20 years of marketplace data onto a global platform

Context
Yemeksepeti — Türkiye's largest food-delivery marketplace and part of Delivery Hero — carried two decades of operational history that needed to move onto Delivery Hero's global platform.
My role
Product owner for the migration. I owned the data migration and the feature-gap analysis between the legacy and new platforms, plus bug triage, friends-and-family testing before go-live, and post-migration follow-up.
Approach
Mapped 20 years of legacy data structures, pinpointed where the new platform fell short of what the business relied on, and sequenced the cutover to keep a live, high-volume marketplace running throughout.
Impact
Two decades of company data moved onto the global platform with a smooth, low-disruption go-live — putting the Turkish business on the same foundation used across Delivery Hero's markets.

Global Product

Ratings & reviews for 20+ marketplaces

Context
Ratings and reviews are how millions of customers decide what to order — and every market has its own habits, languages, and expectations.
My role
Product manager for the global ratings & reviews domain, driving initiatives across 20+ countries spanning APAC, Europe, and MENA.
Approach
Balanced a single, centralized product vision with the realities of local markets — shipping one roadmap that still respects regional differences.
Impact
A more consistent ratings & reviews experience across dozens of markets, built on one shared platform.
