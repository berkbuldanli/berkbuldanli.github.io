Starting a place to write

Why I'm keeping my product writing on my own site — and what I plan to write about here.

I've written on Medium and LinkedIn for a while, and I'll keep cross-posting there. But I wanted a home for this writing that I actually own — something that isn't behind a platform's algorithm or paywall, and that points back to the rest of my work.

So this is that home. Here's roughly what I plan to write about:

  • Marketplaces and migrations. The unglamorous, high-stakes work of moving real systems — and real data — without breaking the business.
  • Search and recommendations. How ranking, relevance, and a bit of ML actually change what customers do.
  • The messy middle of shipping. Discovery, prioritization, and the trade-offs that never make it into the case study.

Posts will be short and practical more often than long and theoretical. If something here is useful — or wrong — tell me.

This is a starter post. Replace or delete it whenever the first real piece is ready.
